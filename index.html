Our Idea
Mealworm
Meet the Mealworm.  Armed with an incredible ability to digest plastic, this arthropod doesn't just remove waste - it also converts it to biodegradeable soil usable for crops. However, these insects can only digest a little bit of plastic at a time - around 36 mg a day. Our team realized that these bugs could use a boost to reach their plastic-eating potential.
